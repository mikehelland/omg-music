OpenMusic.Gallery
Docs

Find, customize, and create music for your project

OpenMusic.Gallery is a musical instrument, a digital audio workstation, and a distribution platform for royalty-free "open music", that is collaborative, decentralized, and open source.

How does it work?

Sound files on the Internet can be arranged into drumbeats and melodies, which can be combined to make larger works of music.

You can click "Remix" on anything in the gallery to make your own version.

Game and Web Development

OMG can be used to compose and play interactive music in JavaScript games and web pages.

Find out more at the Game Dev Page

OMG Live!

  • Connect directly with collaborators instead of sending files back and forth.
  • Bring a live audience in on the action.
  • Remote control with other devices to make multi-screen instruments.
