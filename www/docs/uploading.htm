Files and Uploading in OMG

OMG can use small audio samples from anywhere on the Internet. You can:

  • Use URLs from your own website
  • Use a file-sharing site with public URLs
  • Upload directly to OMG

Every user automatically gets 2 mb of storage on OMG.

That doesn't sound like much, but for short MP3 samples like a a snare drum, it's more than it seems.
